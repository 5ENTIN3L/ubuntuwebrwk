# About Ubuntu Nexus

Ubuntu Nexus is a non-profit, humanitarian organization based in Kenya. We are dedicated to addressing the challenges faced by the youth, including crime, social exclusion, inequality, and injustice.

Our work is guided by an Afrocentric lens, deeply rooted in the spirit of **Ubuntu** — "I am because we are."

We strive to build peaceful and thriving communities through a multi-faceted approach that includes:

-   **Education:** Providing access to knowledge and skills.
-   **Empowerment:** Equipping youth with the tools to shape their own futures.
-   **Advocacy:** Championing the rights and needs of young people.
-   **Prevention:** Proactively working to stop cycles of violence and poverty.
-   **Restoration:** Healing and rebuilding communities affected by conflict and injustice.
