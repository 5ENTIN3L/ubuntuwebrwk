# Project Goals

The primary goal of this project is to develop a comprehensive, secure, and user-friendly website that serves as the digital backbone for Ubuntu Nexus. This platform will support our internal operations and enhance our public engagement.

## Key Objectives:

### 1. Internal Operations Hub
Create a centralized system for all internal team members to streamline workflows, enhance accountability, and improve inter-departmental collaboration.
- **User Management:** Secure login for all staff members with role-based access control.
- **Task & Objective Management:** A system for teams to create, view, update, and log their objectives and tasks.
- **Automated Check-ins:** Allow team members to log their daily or weekly check-ins.
- **Content Creation:** A platform for departments to write and manage articles and other publications.
- **Document Management:** A repository for important documents, including a templated system for meeting minutes for the Executive Director.

### 2. Departmental Integration
Build tailored modules to meet the unique needs of each department while ensuring seamless data flow between them.
- Programs & Community Engagement Department
- Research, Policy & Learning Department
- Partnerships & Resource Mobilization Department
- Operations & Administration Department
- DOCT — Digital Operations & Creative Technology Department
- Social Media, Communications & Branding Department

### 3. Public Engagement & Support
Develop a public-facing interface to communicate our mission, attract support, and engage with the community.
- **Donations:** A secure and simple donation system integrated with Mpesa.
- **Volunteering:** A "Become a Volunteer" application and management system.
- **Communication:** A "Send Us a Message" contact form, with an option for anonymity to encourage open feedback.

### 4. Uphold Organizational Values
Ensure the final product is a digital reflection of the Ubuntu Nexus spirit.
- **Afrocentric & Beautiful:** The design will be modern, visually appealing, and culturally relevant, aiming for an award-winning aesthetic.
- **Secure & Robust:** The system will be built with security as a priority, implementing necessary guardrails and following best practices to ensure data integrity and user privacy.
- **Sustainable:** As a zero-budget project, the technology choices and architecture will prioritize long-term maintainability and low operational costs.
